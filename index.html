Who Am I?

I grew up on the Isle of Dogs in East London. I got to watch Canary Wharf rise from nothing to dominate the London sky line.

Growing up in the East End of London, pie and mash becomes a big part of your life. Saturdays would be a trip to Bethnal Green Road, to walk past McDonalds, KFC and three pie and mash shops to get to the one that my family has been going to for generations. We’d always ask why we couldn’t have McDonalds or KFC to be told that it wasn’t proper food. But I must admit, and don’t tell my mum, that now I’m a parent, I agree!
